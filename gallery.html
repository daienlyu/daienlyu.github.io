Fortune Finder

Fortune Finder is an app dedicated to having a good time in Chinatown and introducing Chinese Culture. All users are welcomed and recommended. The app provides features like tour guides, voucher/coupon claimer, quiz and special activities during holiday session. The app also integrates kid dedicated version which contains special games.

Panasonic Battery Store

A candidate of new UI from Panasonic Battery Inc. The app is a protype dedicated to WeChat mini-program platform. The app utilises gradient colours, portraying visual identifiers for navigating different pages. The lion ambassador is also here for the enjoyful shopping experience.

YWaste Redesigned

YWaste is short for "Why waste?"
The app is a place for food vendors to put on their food which is close to expiration. The initial purpose is to save food insecurity in various areas. The phrase "Fighting Food Waste" used a lot across YWaste's platform and the app itself.

MPD

MPD (Music Playback Deployment) is a solution of intuitive listening and automatic organising for all audiences. When they are always on-the-go, they don't have any more time to organise their music library, therefore the app helps access and play their music faster.

Zero & One Citizens is an interactive program that reflects the status quo of online social media. It circles around the topics like beauty judgement, sense of existence and so-called “friend squad”.

SHOULDN’T

A 3D space to exhibit the objects collected throughout a 3-month academic exploration. Considering most of my works are video formatted and 3D models and based on deconstructionism, I say to myself: why don’t Imake it cinematic and interactive? From there, I decided to build an exhibition themed “surreal”. The name is “SHOULDN’T”.

Vinyl Rhapsody (AR)

Vinyl Rhapsody is an AR interactive app that provides a new experience of showcasing and playing vinyl albums. The concept is to express artists' messages dimensionally and somewhat interact with their audience. It sets out to provide a visual translation to lyrics from the artists.

Yumeland (VR/AV)

Yumeland combines Haptic touch and Room-scale VR for an engaging and immersive player experience. The game design encourages players to use their common sense during gameplay, especially hand haptic feedback.
