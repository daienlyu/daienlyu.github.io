Animation

The Dotte Show E01 - Talkshow

The object is an animation video that tells a story of a character named “Dotte”. The object narrates a journey of an introvert’s mental state through Dotte’s talk show host career and his ability of object morphing. Visually, the object also shows an evolution of User Interface Design through Dotte’s appearance which is injected with a retrospective style, which includes 8-bit, Skeuomorphism, Flat and 3D style. Aesthetically, the object creates a mix of various design styles and intends to make seamless transitions through each style.

Simulation & Simulacra

Present to you by Team Spielberg (Thao, Moses, Kirstin, Daien, Catherine & Pheobe), Simulacra and simulation, a current resurfacing theory by Jean Baudrillard defines the boundaries of simulation. The simulacra being the representation of the real that can overwhelm the idea of reality confirms Baudrillard’s idea that constant remediation of abstractions can bring forth a hyperreal environment that comes from no origin or reality.
