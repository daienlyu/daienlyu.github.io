水星克拉布 (SpaceMan)
朴诚健 & 島

SpaceMan (Mercury Club) is Disco based song, and it is planned to be a sequel of MAVUE, the song I talked about just now. But this time is more imaginary in a club, holding a glass of wine, getting drunk in the beauty of music and lights. For this album, I decided to be more physical but metaphorical by wine glass shape vinyl player, so when you tap the glass, the playback will process.

MA VUE
朴诚健 & 島

As for MAVUE, can be translated to My Sight, which the artist claims, "I cannot get her out of my sight." and describes a wonderful summertime on the beach with romance. We can listen to the joyful beat and imagine the environment. And I started off with that, the first thing I could relate to vaporwave and beach, are holygram and palm tree, so I combine these two elements together and generated a holygram palm tree at the side. And the vinyl player has a followed up makeover as well.

闯入 (Chuang Ru)
朴诚健

Crossing Your Mind aka 闯入. So the singer expresses the fantasies with the girl he wanna spend the rest of his life with. So he imagines an exotic planet and space, which is, he claimed, the wonderland that will never be bothered. Therefore, I designed a place with all the things he wants to have. And I extend the concept into the inner pages with a fast dashing car towards a planet, how romantic he will feel.

Old Days
忘词炫弟儿Sausage (feat. 谭响TanX & Zzqc_Jessie)

Chapter 2, Throwback Thursday. Old Days is a retrospective album, singing about the beautiful childhood of 2000s, therefore, I provided some evidences of the past - my old photos; and some objects that the rapper talks about, such as CDs, Nokia phones, exercise books etc. So I put all of those elements into a slideshow. While listening to the song, there is also a bonus scene where there is a box appearing, and you can put all of those memories into it, like a time capsule.
