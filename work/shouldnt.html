SHOULDN'T - Digital Exhibition of Things That Shouldn't Exist

Daien Lyu

A 3D space to exhibit the objects collected throughout a 3-month academic exploration. Considering most of my works are video formatted and 3D models and based on deconstructionism, I say to myself: why don’t I make it cinematic and interactive? From there, I decided to build an exhibition themed “surreal”. The name is “SHOULDN’T”.
