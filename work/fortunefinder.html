Fortune Finder - Chinese Festival Franchise

Daien Lyu & Larry Lai

Fortune Finder is an app dedicated to having a good time in Chinatown and introducing Chinese Culture. All users are welcomed and recommended. The app provides features like tour guides, voucher/coupon claimer, quiz and special activities during holiday session. The app also integrates kid dedicated version which contains special games.
